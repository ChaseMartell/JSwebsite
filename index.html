Reigning Champion: Ash Ketchum


Ash Ketchum is a legend in the world of Pokémon. He is a Pokémon trainer from Pallet Town, a small town located in the Kanto region. His starter Pokémom was a Pikachu that he received from Porfessor Oak after arriving late at his laboratory. He has travelled across many different regions, but ultimately competes in the league and is one of the most feared trainers. Beware of his Dragonite, Gengar, and Pikachu. They provide quite a punch!
